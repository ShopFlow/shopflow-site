About ShopFlow

ShopFlow started with a simple idea: "Make online shopping smart, smooth and fun."

Anna Batsikadze, a passionate online shopper from Georgia, wanted to create a platform where people could find the best deals, explore brands they love, and do it all — effortlessly.

This site wouldn't exist without the support of Shorena, who believed in the idea from day one and never stopped believing in its success.

ShopFlow is now a fun, helpful space built with purpose — for smarter shopping.
